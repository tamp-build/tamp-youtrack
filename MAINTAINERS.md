## Adding a maintainer

Per ADR 0009 §2.2: by invitation from an existing maintainer, with concurrence from any other existing maintainer if more than one exists. Append a row above with `Joined` set to the date of acceptance.

## Removing a maintainer

Per ADR 0009 §2.2: by self-resignation, by maintainer-team consensus, or by 90 days of unresponsiveness on flagged escalations. Move the row to **Emeritus** with the departure date and a one-line note; do not delete history.
