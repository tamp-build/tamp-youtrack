# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) verbatim. Read the full text at the link above; it governs every issue, pull request, discussion, and interaction in the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that does not align with the Covenant, contact a project maintainer privately:

- Scott Singleton — scott@gscottsingleton.com

Reports are handled confidentially. We follow the enforcement guidelines published with the Covenant: Correction → Warning → Temporary Ban → Permanent Ban, scaled to the impact and pattern of the behavior.

## Scope

The Covenant applies in project spaces (this repository, related repositories, official communication channels) and when an individual is representing the project or its community in public spaces.

## Authority

Project maintainers, as listed in [`MAINTAINERS.md`](MAINTAINERS.md), are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the standards described in the Covenant. Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Covenant in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions per the project's [governance ADR](docs/adr/0009-governance-and-namespace-policy.md).
